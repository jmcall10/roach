road obstacles around charlotte


This map shows the 20 most recent accidents reported in the Charlotte-Meck area.

All information is pulled from CMPD Traffic Twitter and is then cleaned and mapped.

This was created using Python, Flask, and HTML.

This project implements APIs from Twitter and Google.
